Zandvoort aan Zee

Amsterdam Beach

Zandvoort a seaside town just outside of Amsterdam, it's a must visit destination when in The Netherlands. The atmosphere is lively, especially in spring and summer and there is a lot to do! Stroll around the town, the beach and the dunes, take a surfing lesson, visit the Zandvoort Circuit, take a gamble in Holland Casino or relax and grab a drink at a cozy terrace.

A home away from home

Find your favorite place to stay

From boutique hotels and beach cottages to camper spots and apartments, there is something suiteable for everyone. Although there is much to offer, it is wise to book early! That way you can be assured of your favorite spot. Liefs uit Zandvoort has made a list of the nicest places to stay in Zandvoort. I would definitely check it out if I was you! How to get there

And other practical info

Not entirely unimportant, how do you actually get to Zandvoort? The Zandvoort Tourist Office has it all written out, whether you come by public transport or by car. Everything is well explained, so you can not miss it. Besides that there is a lot of useful information to find on their website, such as a map, tourist information and frequently asked questions.
